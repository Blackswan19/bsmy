Interest Rates for Different Amounts

  • For ₹1000: Interest is ₹10 per day
  • For ₹2000: Interest is ₹12 per day
  • For ₹3000: Interest is ₹14 per day
  • For ₹4000: Interest is ₹16 per day
  • For ₹5000: Interest is ₹18 per day
  • For ₹6000: Interest is ₹20 per day
  • For ₹7000: Interest is ₹22 per day
  • For ₹8000: Interest is ₹24 per day
  • For ₹9000: Interest is ₹26 per day

If the amount falls between the listed values, the interest rate will be interpolated accordingly. For example:

  • For an amount between ₹1000 and ₹2000, the interest rate will be between ₹10 and ₹12.
  • For an amount between ₹2000 and ₹3000, the interest rate will be between ₹12 and ₹14.
  • For an amount between ₹3000 and ₹4000, the interest rate will be between ₹14 and ₹16.
  • For an amount between ₹4000 and ₹5000, the interest rate will be between ₹16 and ₹18.
  • For an amount between ₹5000 and ₹6000, the interest rate will be between ₹18 and ₹20.
  • For an amount between ₹6000 and ₹7000, the interest rate will be between ₹20 and ₹22.
  • For an amount between ₹7000 and ₹8000, the interest rate will be between ₹22 and ₹24.
  • For an amount between ₹8000 and ₹9000, the interest rate will be between ₹24 and ₹26.
NOTE: Don't forget to pay on the return date. If any delay's inform us before the date. If delay has taken place extra charges will be added to your interest.
