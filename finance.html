Interest Rates for Different Amounts

  • For ₹1000: Interest is ₹10 per day
  • For ₹2000: Interest is ₹15 per day
  • For ₹3000: Interest is ₹20 per day
  • For ₹4000: Interest is ₹25 per day
  • For ₹5000: Interest is ₹30 per 1.5 day
  • For ₹6000: Interest is ₹35 per 1.5 day
  • For ₹7000: Interest is ₹40 per 1.5 day
  • For ₹8000: Interest is ₹45 per 1.5 day
  • For ₹9000: Interest is ₹50 per 1.5 day

If the amount falls between the listed values, the interest rate will be interpolated accordingly. For example:

  • For an amount between ₹1000 and ₹2000, the interest rate will be between ₹10 and ₹15 per day.
  • For an amount between ₹2000 and ₹3000, the interest rate will be between ₹15 and ₹20 per day.
  • For an amount between ₹3000 and ₹4000, the interest rate will be between ₹20 and ₹25 per day.
  • For an amount between ₹4000 and ₹5000, the interest rate will be between ₹25 and ₹30 per day.
  • For an amount between ₹5000 and ₹6000, the interest rate will be between ₹35 and ₹35 per 1.5 day.
  • For an amount between ₹6000 and ₹7000, the interest rate will be between ₹35 and ₹40 per 1.5 day.
  • For an amount between ₹7000 and ₹8000, the interest rate will be between ₹35 and ₹45 per 1.5 day.
  • For an amount between ₹8000 and ₹9000, the interest rate will be between ₹35 and ₹50 per 1.5 day.
